Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 16, 2026

Durian Merge ("the App") is a casual puzzle game developed by an independent developer. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

1. Data We Collect

  • Nickname — A player-chosen display name stored locally on your device (localStorage). It is also submitted to our online leaderboard if you choose to participate.
  • Game Scores — Your scores are stored locally and, if you participate in the leaderboard, uploaded to Google Firebase Firestore.
  • Advertising ID — Google AdMob may collect your device's advertising identifier to serve personalized or non-personalized ads.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To display your nickname and scores on the global leaderboard.
  • To show advertisements via Google AdMob.
  • To improve the App experience (e.g., game statistics).

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase — Cloud database for leaderboard data.
  • Google AdMob — Advertising service.

4. Data Storage & Security

Local data (nickname, scores, settings) is stored on your device using the browser's localStorage. Online leaderboard data is stored in Firebase Firestore with security rules that restrict access.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Your Rights

You can clear all local data by clearing the App's storage in your device settings. To request removal of your leaderboard data, please contact us at the email below.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Data Deletion Instructions

Effective date: March 29, 2026

Durian Merge ("the App") respects your right to control your personal data. You can delete your data as described below.

1. Delete Local Data (On Your Device)

All game data (nickname, scores, settings, tickets) is stored locally on your device. To delete it:

  1. Open your device Settings
  2. Go to Apps > Durian Merge
  3. Tap Storage > Clear Data

This will remove all locally stored game data immediately.

2. Delete Leaderboard Data (Online)

If you participated in the online leaderboard, your nickname and scores are stored in Google Firebase Firestore. To request deletion of this data, please email us at:

rhswl2135@gmail.com

Include your in-game nickname in the email. We will process your request within 7 business days.

3. Advertising Data

Google AdMob may have collected your advertising identifier. You can reset or opt out of personalized ads through your device settings:

  1. Open Settings > Google > Ads
  2. Tap Reset advertising ID or Delete advertising ID

4. Contact

For any data deletion questions, contact us at:
rhswl2135@gmail.com
